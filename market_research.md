# Why GitHub Data > Market Price (The Developer Alpha)

In the crypto ecosystem, price is a lagging indicator. Developer activity is the only true leading indicator of long-term value. 

### 1. The "Commit" Before the "Pump"
Before a protocol goes viral, its infrastructure is built in public repos. By tracking stars and forks velocity in the first 14 days of a repo's life, we identify projects before they hire a marketing team.

### 2. Developer Velocity as a Proxy for Trust
Scams rarely have high-quality codebases with multiple contributors. A high contributor count (>3) on a new repo is a cryptographic proof of human coordination and technical legitimacy.

### 3. Narrative Frontrunning
By monitoring keywords like `zkML`, `restaking`, and `solana-agent`, AlphaTrace identifies which technical narratives are gaining traction among builders before they reach retail investors on social media.
